Welcome Message

A warm Namaste
from our family
to yours.

"We built Hotel Pun Inn as more than a hotel — we built it as a home where every guest is treated as family."

Namaste and a heartfelt welcome to Hotel Pun Inn. Nestled in the historic neighborhood of Bhotebahal, Kathmandu, our hotel was born from a simple yet profound vision: to offer every traveller a sanctuary where they feel truly at home.

Guided by the timeless Nepali principle of Atithi Devo Bhava — "The Guest is God" — my team and I are dedicated to ensuring that your time with us is seamless, memorable, and deeply personal. Whether you are here to explore the wonders of the Kathmandu Valley or to conduct business in Nepal's capital, we are committed to making every moment of your stay extraordinary.

We look forward to welcoming you personally, and to sharing the warmth, culture, and spirit of our beautiful Nepal with you.

Ramu Pun
Managing Director — Hotel Pun Inn, Kathmandu
